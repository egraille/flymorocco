Paragliding in Morocco

With the Atlas range crossing a great part of the country, Morocco has the most beautiful and rich landscape to discover from the sky.

Morocco is a superb winter destination for thermal hungry pilot when the skies are dull and grey back home. Morocco offers sun, blue skies, thermals and tagines better than anywhere else. From high moutains, flat lands and dune flying, every pilot will find their own favorite playground. Morocco is only at couple of hours flight from European countries.
