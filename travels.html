Travelling to Morocco

All the information you need to consider to travel to Morocco. Update on Covid-19

COVID-19 UPDATE
Morocco is in a state of emergency. Masks must be worn in public places.
Travels are allowed following specific requirements detailed below.
  1. Negative PCR test of 72 hours prior departure date
  2. Hotel reservation OR invitation from a Moroccan company

Morocco is a gateway to Africa and a country of dizzying diversity. Here you'll find epic mountain ranges, ancient cities, sweeping deserts – and warm hospitality.

Following your nationality, visa will be automatically granted at the border for 90 days. Consult the Moroccan Embassy of your home country for more informations. 

No specific vaccins are required in this country.

Tap water is drinkable in big cities but in countryside and villages, it is often coming from local streams without treatments. It is advised to consume bottled water while being in the countryside.

Dress code - the dress code is particularly relaxed, included for woman. Morocco is very open to tourism and locals are used to see different outfits especially in touristic places. The only exception will be when visiting sacred and religious places where there'll be a stricter dress code where both men and women need to cover up.
